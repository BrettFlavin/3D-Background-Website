Your Tank Will Thank You!

We've all been there...re-arranging tank decor for the 3rd time this week only to decide we don't like it again!

With our 3D-backgrounds you'll never have to worry again! - You can now get that unique, natural, and amazingly beautiful look that will also ensure your fish and plants stay happy and healthy in their underwater forever home for years to come! We make 3D-backgrounds both thick and thin. Generally, the thicker are more expensive and here's a few differences to help you decide:

The thick-background has more details, extra rocks, and will provide a bit more cover and hiding spaces for your fish. This can help smaller fish and shy fish to feel more secure and comfortable.

The thin-background is for those who would liek to take up as little space as possible inside the tank, yet still get that natural and unique underwater look. This type is usually best for bigger fish that would prefer the open swimming space.

Our 3D-backgrounds are made to be virtually maintenance free and offers both strength and flexibility by using a non-toxic coating that has been tested in underwater environments from -78-140 F. This coating also allows the background to easily be wiped cleaned if desired.
